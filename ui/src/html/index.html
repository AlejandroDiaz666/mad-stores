Welcome to MAD Stores MAD Stores is a Decentralized, Anonymous Ethereum-based Marketplace.
And it works differently from every other online marketplace.
  • In MAD Stores you will not know the identity of your seller!

  • Decentralized means that there is no managing authority to prevent cheating!

  • And yet, using an escrow system, called MAD Escrow, it's possible to ensure that most transactions will complete successfully.
MAD stands for Mutually Assurred Destruction. In every purchase transaction both parties place a bond into escrow. If either party cheats, then both parties lose their deposits!
The next few screens will fully explain how a MAD Escrow works.

Mad Escrow

MAD Escrow
  • When you purchase an item in MAD Stores you will deposit 150% of the price into an escrow account.
  • Automatically, the seller will also deposit a bond into the escrow account, equal to 50% of the purchase price.
  • All funds are released from the MAD Escrow when the buyer confirms satisfactory delivery of the product.
  • If the product was not as advertised, or was if it was not delivered, then the buyer and seller are encouraged to communicate, using Turms Anonymous Message Transport, to work out a satisfactory resolution.
  • In case no resolution can be reached, the buyer has the option to "burn" the escrow. That is, to destroy all the funds that are held in the Escrow.

Dashboard (Ending an Escrow)

When the seller approves your purchase you will see the approval icon . Click this icon to see the seller's commitment to deliver the product. If you reply, then clicking on the approval icon will show the most recent message relating to the approval. Once the seller approves the purchase the escrow is "locked". That means the escrow can't be canceled.

Click the Release icon, , to confirm satisfactory delivery of the item.
Or click the Burn icon, in the unlikely event that you are completely unsatisfied, and you are unable to come to a resolution with the seller.

When you approve this purchase, you are commiting to deliver the product within a fixed amount of time. If the buyer does not receive the product within the specified time, he will likely "burn" the escrow, causing you to lose the product payment plus your bond funds (50% of the product price). Creating your own MAD Escrow Store is easy!
Simply enter the name of your store; a description of your store or the services you offer; and a logo. When you are all done click the 'register-my-store' (or Re-register-my-store) button. Once your store is registered you can begin adding products.

Simply enter a brief, and also a more complete description of the product; enter the price per item, and the number of items you have avilable; and upload a photo of the product. When you are all done click the 'add-this-product' button.
